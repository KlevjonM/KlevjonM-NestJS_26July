About Lions

Lions are majestic big cats known for their strength, courage, and social behavior. They are the second-largest living cat species after tigers.

Habitat

Lions primarily inhabit grasslands and savannas. They can be found in sub-Saharan Africa and a small population exists in the Gir Forest of India.

Behavior

Lions are the only cats that live in groups, called prides. They are apex predators and are known for their cooperative hunting strategies.

Conservation

Lion populations have been declining due to habitat loss and human conflict. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure their survival in the wild.
